About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game inspired by popular rogue-lite games. Your objective is simple: survive as long as possible against increasingly difficult waves of monsters. As you play, you'll collect experience points to level up and choose new abilities, weapons, and passive bonuses.

The game features simple controls - just use WASD or arrow keys to move your character, and your weapons will automatically target the nearest enemies. Strategy comes from your choice of upgrades and how you position yourself among the monster hordes.
